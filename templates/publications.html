Publications

To cite OnToology please use the first reference listed below:
  • Automating ontology engineering support activities with OnToology. Alobaid, A., Garijo, D., Poveda-Villalón, M., Santana-Pérez, I., Fernández-Izquierdo, A., & Corcho, O. (2018). Journal of Web Semantics.

    You can download the bib entry (here)

  • OnToology, a tool for collaborative development of ontologies. Alobaid, A., Garijo, D., Poveda-Villalón, M., Pérez, I. S., & Corcho, O. (2015, July). In ICBO.
  • OnToology: An online tool for ontology documentation and evaluation Alobaid, A. (2015). (Master dissertation, ETSI_Informatica).
